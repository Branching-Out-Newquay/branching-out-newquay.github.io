BRANCHING OUT

Code and Makers Club

Growing Opportunities Through Coding and Making

Introduction

Branching Out is a code and makers club, based in Newquay, designed to empower and inspire people through coding and hands-on making. Our mission is to foster creativity, problem-solving, and digital literacy in a supportive, inclusive and sustainable environment. The club is open to all skill levels who are curious about technology and innovation and aims to meet a diverse range of needs including:

  • school-age children
  • career changers
  • job seekers
  • people exploring new hobbies and creative outlets.
