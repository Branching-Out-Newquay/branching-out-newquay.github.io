Events

We currently run open sessions once a month at the Newquay Orchard. These are free and no booking is required. If you are under 16 you need to bring a responsible adult with you but they can have a go at coding and making as well!

We have laptops, micro:bits and other equipment you can borrow but you can also bring along your own. If you are not sure where to start we have a range of fun exercises to work through. We've had all ages, from about 5 years to over 70, trying these and everyone comes away smiling. If you've got something particular you want to learn or have a project you are working through give us a challenge. You can also help us improve some of things we have been building for the club.

Check out our coding and making pages to find out more.
